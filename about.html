Finley Banks is a New York City Designer and photographer. Currently pursuing a B.F.A. in Communication Design with a double minor in Photography and Design Studies.

At Parsons, she has gone on to found her own magazine, the locale creator. An on-campus magazine that focuses on uniting students across multiple mediums and forming authentic connections.

Previously, Finley has worked for multiple on-campus organizations at Syracuse University, such as University Girl Magazine, Zipped Magazine, and What Everyone Really Wants Radio.

In Finley's free time, she enjoys reading, yoga, knitting, making linocuts, and being with her friends and family.
